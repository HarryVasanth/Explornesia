Process

of our game design...

The Explornesia Trilogy is an outcome of our Game Design course at the University of Madeira. The whole process from ideation to delivery just took only four weeks to complete. During this time we developed three different games based on three different mediums: A traditional TABLETOP BOARD GAME, A DIGITAL game and HYBRID game which encompasses both tabletop and digital aspects. Each game have its own unique storyline, mechanics and aesthetics.


  • November 21, 2016

    Design Concept

    We decided to divide the team into three groups to address the all three mediums. We came up with conceptual material such as concept art, storyline, game genres, etc.

  • November 28, 2016

    Paper Prototype

    Prototyping all three games in paper followed by quick iterations to adjust mechanics, aesthetics and storyline, etc.

  • December 05, 2016

    Development

    Began to develop the games with the insights gained from paper prototypes along with supervised feedback from our tutor Prof. Sergi Bermúdez i Badia.

  • December 12, 2016

    Public Playtest

    We conducted our public playtest session at the Madeira Interactive Technologies Institute; All three games were playtested by 20+ participants and the sessions were very helpful in ironing out all the shortcoming of our games.

The Age of Explornesia

Get your ship together and get started!

Explore the Macaronesia by playing our fun and immersive board game. In this game your objective is to collect the necessary resources to build your own ship and obtain the king's approval to commence your journey. This game progressively becomes competitive. You can use the art of negotiation with your opponents or use the appropriate mystery cards at the appropriate time to progress quicker than your opponents. Sit back, relax , and enjoy this tabletop experience.

The Journey

Wield the lie detector to find who are the spies among you!

The objective of the Portuguese nationals is to get rid of all the Spanish crew members or accomplish the minimum number of quests required to win. For the Spanish faction, they should get rid of all Portuguese crew members or fail the maximum amount of quests required. In this game you will have access to a magical bracelet (smartwatch) to figure out if your crew is lying to you (about their nationality).

Game Elements


Smartwatch

The smartwatch will be used to acquire the physiological data (heart rate, etc.)

Smartphone

Visualize the smartwatch data as heart rate variability based lie detector/nervousness meter.

Vote Card

The players who are sent on a quest must vote by using this card in order to determine if the succession of the quest.

Role Card

Determines the role of the player in the ship (Captain or Crew). Only one person can be the captain at any given time.

Nationality Card

Determines the player's Nationality (Portuguese / Spanish).

Quest Card

Initiates a Quest. The outcome of the Quest is determined by the player votes

The Battle

A sea of mayhem with your friends!

In the first phase your objective is to defeat your opponent via an epic multiplayer battle between Portuguese and Spanish ships. In the second part you will be tactically challenged to destroy your enemy's fortifications by strategically placing your own fortification. While doing so, you would get an opportunity or two to glance at your opponents tactics via a spying binoculars. Use this for your advantage and grasp your chance for glory!
